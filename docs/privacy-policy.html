Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ScrollWisdom ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

ScrollWisdom stores user preferences (such as selected topics, language, and notification settings) and streak data locally on your device using UserDefaults. No personal data is collected or transmitted to external servers.

2. Push Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications to remind you of your daily wisdom. This is entirely optional and can be disabled at any time in iOS Settings.

3. Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. ScrollWisdom does not collect, process, or store any payment information. All billing is managed through your Apple ID account.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed.

6. Children's Privacy

ScrollWisdom does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
