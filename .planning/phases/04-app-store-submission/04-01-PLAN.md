  <action>
User must complete these steps in App Store Connect (https://appstoreconnect.apple.com):

**Step A: Verify Paid Applications Agreement (CRITICAL -- do this first)**
1. Go to **Business** (top nav) > **Agreements**
2. Confirm "Paid Applications" agreement status is **Active**
3. If not active: sign the agreement, complete tax forms, add banking information
4. Without this, subscription products CANNOT be created

**Step B: Create App ID (per D-01: Lifestyle category)**
1. Go to **Apps** (top nav) > click **+** button > **New App**
2. Fill in:
   - **Platforms:** iOS
   - **Name:** ScrollWisdom
   - **Primary Language:** English (U.S.)
   - **Bundle ID:** Select the Bundle ID matching your Xcode project (com.kirillmagerya.ScrollWisdom or similar)
   - **SKU:** scrollwisdom-v1
   - **User Access:** Full Access
3. Click **Create**
4. After creation, go to **App Information** (left sidebar):
   - **Category:** Lifestyle (per D-01)
   - **Content Rights:** Select "Does not contain third-party content" or declare appropriately
   - **Age Rating:** Click "Set Up" -- answer all questions "None" (no violence, gambling, adult content, etc.)
5. Click **Save**

In App Store Connect, navigate to **your app** > **Subscriptions** (left sidebar).

**Step A: Create Subscription Group (per D-08)**
1. Click **+** next to "Subscription Groups"
2. Reference Name: **ScrollWisdom Premium**
3. Click **Create**

**Step B: Add Monthly Product (per D-07)**
1. Inside "ScrollWisdom Premium" group, click **+** next to "Subscriptions"
2. Reference Name: **Monthly Premium**
3. Product ID: **com.scrollwisdom.premium.monthly** (MUST match exactly -- copy-paste this)
4. Click **Create**
5. On the product page:
   - **Subscription Duration:** 1 Month
   - **Subscription Price:** Click "Add Subscription Price" > select $3.99 USD (Apple auto-generates other currencies)
   - **Introductory Offers:** Click **+** > Type: **Free** > Duration: **3 Days** > Number of Periods: **1**
   - **Localization (English):** Click **+** > Display Name: **Monthly Premium** > Description: **All topics, unlimited saves, no ads**
   - **Localization (Russian):** Click **+** > Display Name: **Месячная подписка** > Description: **Все темы, безлимитные сохранения, без рекламы**
6. Click **Save**

**Step C: Add Yearly Product (per D-07)**
1. Inside "ScrollWisdom Premium" group, click **+** next to "Subscriptions"
2. Reference Name: **Yearly Premium**
3. Product ID: **com.scrollwisdom.premium.yearly** (MUST match exactly -- copy-paste this)
4. Click **Create**
5. On the product page:
   - **Subscription Duration:** 1 Year
   - **Subscription Price:** Click "Add Subscription Price" > select $29.99 USD
   - **Introductory Offers:** Click **+** > Type: **Free** > Duration: **3 Days** > Number of Periods: **1**
   - **Localization (English):** Click **+** > Display Name: **Yearly Premium** > Description: **All topics, unlimited saves, no ads -- save 37%**
   - **Localization (Russian):** Click **+** > Display Name: **Годовая подписка** > Description: **Все темы, безлимитные сохранения, без рекламы -- экономия 37%**
6. Click **Save**

**Step D: Verify Both Products**
1. Go back to Subscriptions overview
2. Both products should show status **Ready to Submit** (or "Waiting for Review")
